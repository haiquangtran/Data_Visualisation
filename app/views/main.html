What influences a parent's expectations for their child?


Children can feel pressured when their parent's have such high expectations of them. The following visualisations display the parent's educational expectations of their children. Specifically, it displays a trend in the data where parent's that earn less, tend to have high educational expectations for their child. Another interesting finding is that fathers tend to expect more from their children as opposed to mothers.
